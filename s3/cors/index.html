About Cricket

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players. It is a popular sport in many countries, including India, Australia, England, and South Africa.

Cricket Rules

  • Each team consists of 11 players.
  • The match is played with a bat and a ball.
  • The team that scores the most runs wins the game.
  • The game is divided into innings, and each team gets a chance to bat and bowl.
